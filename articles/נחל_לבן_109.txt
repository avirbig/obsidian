A Note on the Obsidian Finds from the Pre-Pottery
Neolithic B Site 109 Near Nahal Lavan, Negev
F. BURIAN and E. FRIEDMAN*
Site 109 is situated between Nahal Lavan and Nahal Ruth, near their con
into Nahal Nizzana (IG 0993/0370). The site covers a few dunams, and is f

The finds include a few thousand tools of flint, stone and obsidian and
ornaments. The main finds include 2023 arrowheads which are all of the Helwan
point type (type 4 [Burian and Friedmann 1979]), 296 axes, as well as retouched
blades and awls. Also collected at this site were 356 obsidian artifacts with a total
weight of 452 grams (table 1). The obsidian appears in a number of colors, from
black to light brown and transparent.
